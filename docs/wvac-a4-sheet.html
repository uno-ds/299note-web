Nurturing pet health,
together with family and vets.

A pet healthcare platform connecting owners and veterinary clinics through health data and shared stories.

Three pillars

Health Timeline

Vaccinations, check-ups, daily observations — all shared between family and vet in chronological order.

Clinic Integration

Not a replacement for existing EMR systems — a patient-facing layer that respects your workflow.

Rainbow Bridge

After a pet passes, health records and family memories are woven into a single memorial narrative.

For veterinary clinics

299note does not replace your EMR system. It operates as a patient-facing layer on top of whatever you already use. Integration is as simple as staff uploading test results. If you are interested, please reach out: hello@299note.com.
