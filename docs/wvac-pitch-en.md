## 1-minute version

> Hi, I'm building 299note — a pet healthcare platform
> connecting owners and veterinary clinics.
>
> Over 9 million households in Japan care for pets today.
> Pets have become family, and awareness of their health
> has never been higher.
>
> Yet the medical records kept at veterinary clinics
> and the daily observations of owners still live in
> separate places. This disconnect limits the precision of
> preventive care — and the quality of the time families
> spend saying goodbye.
>
> 299note **does not replace existing EMR systems**.
> It operates as a patient-facing layer on top of whatever
> the clinic already uses. Integration is as simple as
> staff uploading test results.
>
> One feature that's unique to us is **Rainbow Bridge Memorial**
> — after a pet passes, health records and family memories
> are woven into a single memorial narrative.
>
> We're an independent project based in Yokohama, with
> corporate registration planned for 2026.
>
> If you're interested, you can reach us at 299note.com.

## Three phrases to memorize

1. **"A pet healthcare platform connecting owners and veterinary clinics."** (one-line definition)
2. **"A patient-facing layer on top of existing EMR systems."** (B2B reassurance)
3. **"Stories that live past the rainbow bridge."** (differentiator)
